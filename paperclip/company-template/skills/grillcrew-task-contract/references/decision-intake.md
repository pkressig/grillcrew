# ChatGPT to Paperclip decision intake

Record these fields before creating implementation work:

1. Decision summary and Product Owner approval evidence.
2. User/business reason and expected outcome.
3. Feature, step, priority, goal, and dependencies.
4. Exact scope and explicit exclusions.
5. Binding decisions and source documents.
6. Open questions and the person authorized to answer each.
7. Acceptance criteria and required test evidence.
8. Documentation/update obligations.
9. Workspace mode and base/target refs.
10. Required skills, executor, review stages, and final approver.
11. GitHub, deployment, and cleanup authority; default to no authority when absent.

If approval evidence, scope, or controlling product sources conflict, create a planning/decision issue and do not create implementation children.
